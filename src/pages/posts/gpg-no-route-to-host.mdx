1. Open `~.gnupg/gpg.conf` and add the following line:

```bash frame="code" title="gpg.conf"
keyserver hkp://keyserver.ubuntu.com:80
```

If the above step doesn't work, [check (and change) your DNS](https://howto.zemn.xyz/posts/dns-linux-how-to-change)
